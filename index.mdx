## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up and upload your transcript">
    Create an account and upload your unofficial transcript PDF. Sift uses Claude AI to automatically parse your completed courses and grades.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your preferences">
    Customize your recommendation weights — prioritize easy A's, highly-rated professors, or core requirements.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get personalized recommendations">
    View ranked course recommendations for your target semester, complete with grade distributions, professor ratings, and prerequisite validation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Plan your semester">
    Add courses to your plan and track your progress toward degree completion.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Built For Texas A&M CS Students

Sift pulls real data from:

- **Anex** — Grade distribution data for every CSCE course and professor
- **RateMyProfessors** — Professor ratings, difficulty scores, and would-take-again percentages
- **Degree Plans** — Official CS degree requirements with prerequisite chains and equivalency rules
